## Actionable or Direct Actions:
The following below are steps that can be taken immediately.

### User Layer (GBP Admin) or Direct Business Owner: 
- Take over the Map Account (if not already).
- Collect or request and Reply to Reviews. Review requests must be based on transaction facts.
- Create "What's New" Info Posts by Owner. For scenario examples, you can use the reference from the "GBP What's New Info Scenario Examples by Owner" section below.
- Direct Visitors or Clients to UGC/Post (office visits, unit photos).
- Complete parameters on GBP (especially NAP, primary services and products, business description). Including working hours and days, entry routes from the lobby if your office is located in an apartment. 
- Connect GBP to the Web and Social Media Accounts, prioritize the website.
- Connect Business Entities (distributors, resellers, branches) via GBP Post.
- Perform Routine GBP Posts or according to factual events (transactions, business connections, new products or services), case studies, installations.

Important Note: 
- Avoid Editing or Adding 4-5 parameters at once.
- Try to leave a minimum interval of 3 days for each edit or post.  
- Do not reply to reviews with copy-paste or identical templates.
- Avoid excessive editing on photos or videos; original, unedited photos or videos are actually much better.
- If there is a standard Business default template, accompany it with real photos on the next slide.

### Field Team Layer: 
- EEAT History Data: track installations that have already been deployed for major clients. 
- Documentation of ongoing and upcoming transactions, operations, and events.
- From the 2 points above, what needs to be collected for the Content Team is the description of activities as well as active GPS geo-tagging photos or videos.
- Additional tips: include unique sentences that match the field situation, e.g., complexity, installation angles, unit types and features, case studies.
- Collect content for the Related Team (GBP Admin, Content Team, Web/SEO Team) to be processed and published. 
- The above activities are carried out periodically, scheduled, and reflexively according to field situations.
- Include Unit Technical Data as Information Gain.

### Social Media Admin or Content Creator Team Layer: 
- Create Content based on the scope below or the instructions in the masterplan. 
- It is recommended that content be based on utility facts or products that actually exist on the official website.
- Avoid excessive editing, use AI-generated covers if necessary, but accompany them with real proof photos/videos if available on the next slide.
- Full caption, keyword awareness in the title and let the rest be natural within the description. 
- Writing recommendations can refer to IMRAD.
- Maintain content purity based on project locations or case studies per city with utility facts.

### Company Layer
After receiving the Audit and Masterplan documents, the steps that can be taken are: 
- Analyze the relevance of the received documents and try to align the methods with business strategies and policies. 
- Make a decision whether to use an Agency or have it handled by the Internal Team.
- Prepare a budget and select a digital agency or partner. 
- Support the movements of the Marketing Team operating without an Agency.
- Support agency operations, perform privacy filtering, and grant approval for content or information to be circulated. 
- Provide additional parameters and expectations based on your Brand's business strategy which is tactical and dynamic, following market movements.
- Ensure business legality, transactions, and operations are synchronized with digital visibility.

### Web Developer Layer
- Ensure the loading speed performance of the main website is clean and responsive.
- Install entity schema markup (JSON LD) that is synchronized with NAP data from the Google Business Profile.
- Create a Web based on the structure existing within the content scope and the framework in the masterplan. 
- Sort between articles that are urgent and routine completions.
- Urgent Web Articles mean they possess high EEAT Value and are Sources of Helpful Information.
- If Urgent articles comply with the Search Ecosystem T.O.S, then with a sporty strategy, it can have a major impact on SERP. 

### SEO Agency and IT or SEO Practitioner Layer: 
- Study the audit and masterplan results and align them with the Customer's Vision (expectations, parameters, targets, privacy constraints, timeline).
- Use all expertise and combine it with the parameters above into a single processed SEO Strategy. 
- Execute the SEO Strategy, delegate tasks to the relevant teams, perform monitoring and audits. 
- Maintain the work cycle and client brand visibility performance and report them routinely.
- Monitor indexing movements and character, and guard compliance with Google's Terms of Service.

## Quick Scope for Main Structure of Content Topics:
### Main Content Notes: 
- Pay attention to and protect privacy points, for example vehicle license plates, personal documents, customer phone numbers and emails in documents, and company secrets in general.
- Keyword Awareness is not Keyword Research, meaning let keywords become natural as in their utility functions and case studies. 
- Primary keywords such as niche, market niche, industrial sector, product lines should ideally be placed in the post title and spread naturally.

### Main Scope: 
- Main Business Identity (name, history, location, experience, capacity, competence).
- Main Product Line of the Business. 
- Target Market and Regional.
- Product and Service Iteration. 
- Field Activities and Case Studies Iteration.
- Business or Service Events. 
- Service Education on the CSR (Customer Self Repair) layer or minor damages.
- Minor Troubleshooting education.

- Factory Tour and Case Studies of Cover or Panel materials and their applications in industry or locations (indoor, outdoor).
- National and International Business Connections and Indonesian Branches (regional), can also be with suppliers.
- Field Documentation (delivery, unboxing, QC Test, Installation, as well as handover).
- Documentation of Repair Visits or Unit Maintenance Contracts.  
- Documentation of Delivery, Unboxing Hardware Units, Unit Testing/QC Test, up to Project Completion Handover.

## GBP What's New Info Scenario Examples by Owner

Applied Scenario Format for Publishing Fact-Based Digital Asset Content. 
Please note the unique level of information in titles or similar contexts. 
On each Article and GBP Post, try to ensure there is a real proof Photo/Video from an active GPS HP device and Geo-Tagging File Meta Tags.
Ideally at least 3 photo files, for example: before, process, and after. 
Reminded again not to perform excessive editing; raw/real photos or videos are highly recommended. This is different from Social Media content.

### Product
- product name, main features, price range, specs, best applied scenario.
- front, back, and powered-on unit photos. Unit video.
- main benefits.
- product availability, stock if any, availability city, include distributor name, CTA. 

### Service
- service name, e.g., delivery service or additional installation.
- list of location applications or industrial sectors, differences between services or cities. 
- customer benefits from this service. 
- Photo/video proof of work ever performed, CTA. 

### Case Study 
- title, main problem, solution provided, benefits for customers or industry. 
- examples of Case Studies: Difficult Installation Locations, Specific Heights, Room or Area Humidity, Layout position with sunlight, Width of Installation Point Space. 
- Can also be delivered using the IMRAD writing style.

### Field Documentation
Just like other posting scenario points, this Field Documentation is very important as additional material for customer consideration.
- Title, Client, Unit Type, main challenge. 
- Unit Unboxing process, installation, and finishing. 
- Everything would be excellent if there is a before-after.
- Can be expanded to unit types, different locations, different industrial sectors.

### New Product Launch 
- Product Name, SKU, Main Advantages, Evolution or features that exceed the previous series.
- Created for what specific sector, its industrial benefits.
- Goods availability and Available Cities, warranty features and service guarantees.
- CTA.

### Launching Event Internal
- Usually related to product launching, collaboration, gathering, product tour.
- Event Schedule, Event Purpose, participating entities.
- Detailed breakdown of event program, public benefits of the event.
- Note: very good to include parameters according to facts e.g. industrial sector, city, client brand, event location.

### New Business Relationship Registration
- Related to, among other things, the introduction of new entities: branches, distributors, Authorized service partners or resellers, procurement partners. 
- Or project contracts: local government, city government, SOEs (BUMN) or State-Owned Industries. Partnership with National Advertisers or National Media.
- Command Center on an industrial or Police scale.

### Incoming Goods Logistics or Outgoing Shipments.
This is important because it includes Operational Scale and Business Competence. 
- Product origin, number of units, destination whether for restocking or for specific projects. 
- How is the logistics process, unboxing tests, and testing procedures for units when newly received.
- Power-on test for 1 unit or several units.

### Out Of The Box by Onsite Team or Content Writer
- Direct tips and tricks from the Installer team regarding differences in Installation terrain and height. 
- Basic Tips and Tricks on how to care for units.
- Condition of the installed unit when turned on during the day or at night.
- Participate in the installation or repair process without disrupting the process and focus on documenting activities.
- That the above application will greatly enrich articles or assets because even 1 product line will generate a lot of Helpful-Content.

### Out Of The Box by Data Analyst Team
- Product and Spec Comparison. 
- Comparison in terms of product base materials, both frame and display.
- Price Difference Comparison between product lines one and two in certain use-case sectors. 
- In-depth Analysis of Units and Weather Factors in terms of product frame materials and LCD panel materials.

Important Tips: 
That every post with valid proof and delivery according to Google's Terms of Service will automatically lock the algorithmic trust function on your brand.
Parameters referred to: City, Niche, Product Lines, Industrial Sector.
